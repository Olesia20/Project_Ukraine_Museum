About the Honchar Museum in Kyiv

Mission

The Ivan Honchar Museum National Centre of Folk Culture is an open art and culture, educational, scientific and recreational space. It fosters knowledge and experience in the field of traditional folk culture. In contemporary society, the museum functions as the caretaker of the past and the leader of new visions and ideas.
